About Local Movies


Finding Movies Easier Since 2019

Local Movies was founded by Carl Koenig, Kevin Kaiser, Rob Leslie, and Smit Patel. Our goal is to make visiting your favorite movie theaters simple and straightforward.

Using our website to find a movie theater is free of charge. We hope you have a positive experience using our website!
